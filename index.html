About

Let's take a look at our story

  • 2009

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The story begun with a dream along with our overwhelming passion for motorcycles. This is the year when I first dreamt of having a motorbike of my own. I chose my motorcycle to be my ally towards the journey to escape oblivion

  • March 2011

    A Dream Came True

    This is actually the time when what we are today started taking shape. We discovered how actually riding gives the pleasure, not one bit less how we pictured it in our dreams. This is when we decided how the rest of our lives will be shaped, on two wheels!

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    Make your passion your occupation- Maybe we took this saying way to seriously! This is the time when we actually was trying to come up with an idea how we can do that. This is when the idea of creating an e-commerce site came up. The entire idea of an online store furnished with imported top-class goods that are necessary for adventure seeking bikers was born at this point!

  • July 2018

    Putting Heart and Soul

    This is when we actually started working with motorcycles and the riders. First, we tried to identify the needs of the riders. Us being riders ourselves has given a huge advantage in identifying the needs of the adventure seekers. Using this e-commerce site as a bridge between us and the other riders, we started importing goods and and showcasing them in our website for you to find in a click!

YZF R15 V3

Arguably the most desired motorbike in Bangladesh

This beast is most probably the most desired motorcycle in Bangladesh. Compared to it's previous version (V2), it gets a new and slightly larger 155cc liquid-cooled four-valve motor with more performance in the low range. The softer front suspension soaks up bad road surfaces well while the rear suspension seems to be a bit too reactive and bouncy over bumps and potholes.

  • Category: Sports
  • Engine displacement : 155 cc
  • Power : 19.3 PS @ 10000 rpm

  • Category: Sports
  • Engine displacement : 149.16 cc

Suzuki gsxr 150R

Speedstar on the move

It uses a fuel injected, water cooled motor and weighs just 126 kg, without fluids. The aerodynamic design is shared with the GSX-R125 and seems fully inspired from the liter class GSX-R1000

  • Category: Sports
  • Engine displacement : 147.3 cc
  • Power : 18.90 Bhp @ 10,500 rpm

Khardungla Pass

It's not just about the machine

We're not here just to talk about the machine you'll choose. We believe that the journey is equally important too!! To summit the Khardungla Pass on a motorcycles has been the dream of any pro biker since childhood. Just by looking at pictures of the ride, countless riders got motivated at the first place to go for a motorcycle!!

  • Place: India
  • Speciality: Highest motorable road on earth
  • Preferred ride: Royal Enfield

Ladakh

A trip to cherish

Ladakh is literally considered the heaven on earth. And what could be better than exploring heaven on your favourite machine!! Situated in India, Ladakh is also very close to Pakistan. One bike trip here and you'll never run out of stories to tell throughout your entire life!!
